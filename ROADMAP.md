## Risk Factors for Roadmap

### What Could Delay Progress

1. **Regulatory Changes** - New regulations may require pivots
2. **TronScan Appeal** - Rejection would significantly impact timeline
3. **Market Conditions** - Bear markets reduce adoption
4. **Technical Issues** - Unforeseen bugs or vulnerabilities
5. **Resource Constraints** - Limited development resources

### Mitigation Strategies

- Transparent communication about delays
- Flexible timeline adjustments
- Community engagement for support
- Conservative milestone estimation
